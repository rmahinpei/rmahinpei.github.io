Here are some of the projects that I've taken on over the years, many of which are simply my attempts at applying newly gained skills. Feel free to see the source code for all these projects by visiting my GitHub.

ISB Finances Website

  • An introductory financial website aiming to educate young adults on finances by providing a compilation of learning resources, useful terminology, and a budgeting calculator.
  • Collaborated with four other students via GitHub and created project with React on Visual Studio Code.
  • Coded the website template as well as the Home, Resources, and Glossary pages.

Romina Mahinpei's Personal Website

  • A summary of my programming journey and the learning resources I used with the intention of inspiring other students to explore coding.

Weight Loss Simulator

  • Coded a weight loss simulator with Python on Jupyter Notebooks under the supervision of Dr. Leah-Edelstein Keshet (UBC Math Department).
  • Used simulator to investigate the effects of a strict calorie reduction on body weight and presented findings at the Multidisciplinary Undergraduate Research Conference (MURC 2021).

Battleships Game
