Projects

Home | Projects | Learning | Course Notes

Most of my projects use computational and/or mathematical techniques to address open-ended, interdisciplinary problems of personal or social significance. As of right now, the majority of my projects use such techniques for an education or health-related purpose, although I am open and even hoping to expand my target in the future.

Education

  • StudyBuddy Application: A Java application consisting of both a console-based component and a GUI component that aims to allow university students to develop effective study habits. StudyBuddy aims to achieve this goal by encouraging students to identify exam-worthy subjects and then complete a minimum of three practice problems per each identified topic.
  • ISB Finances Website: A React JS website that aims to educate students on financial wellness through a compilation of financial resources and a budgeting calculator.
  • Minimizing the Use of Polyethene inside Paper Coffee Cups: A proposal of a mathematical method for determining the dimensions that would minimize the amount of polyethylene plastic covering the internal surface of a coffee cup with the intention of reducing the amount of plastic that end up in landfills.
  • A Comparative Analysis of the Gini Index: An analysis of three of the earliest developed methods for the derivation and evaluation of the Gini index, a global metric summarizing a region's income disparity into a single value, emphasizing the importance of acknowledging the method used to derive the Gini Index to ensure consistency and to allow a valid interpretation.

Health

  • Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) Simulator: A Python simulator program based on a deterministic compartmental model that tracks the blood alcohol concentration of simulated subjects following alcohol consumption in the hopes of encouraging the population to learn more about the impacts of their drinking behaviours on their health.
  • Weight Loss Simulator: A Python simulator program based on the most recent deterministic model of weight loss that trakcs the body weight of simulated subjects following restricted caloric intakes in the hopes of highlighting the impacts of restricted caloric intakes on health.
  • OnTrack Application: An interactive console-based health application written in Python that allows user to track food consumption, physical activity, and water intake in the hopes of promoting a healthy lifestyle.
